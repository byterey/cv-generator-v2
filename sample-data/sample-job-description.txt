Senior DevSecOps Engineer

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior DevSecOps Engineer to join our platform engineering team.
You will be responsible for securing our CI/CD pipelines, automating security testing,
and driving vulnerability remediation across our cloud infrastructure.

Requirements
- 5+ years of experience in DevOps, DevSecOps, or application security
- Strong experience with CI/CD tools (GitLab CI, Jenkins, GitHub Actions)
- Experience with SAST, DAST, and SCA tooling
- Cloud experience (Azure preferred, AWS or GCP acceptable)
- Container security and Kubernetes experience
- Proficiency in at least one scripting language (Python, Bash, or PowerShell)
- Familiarity with OWASP Top 10 and vulnerability management
- Incident response and on-call experience

Nice to Have
- Experience with Ansible, Terraform, or other IaC tools
- Security certifications (CISSP, Azure Security, or similar)
- Experience in regulated industries (finance, healthcare, government)
- Threat modelling experience
- SIEM and monitoring tool experience (Splunk, Azure Application Insights)

What You'll Do
- Own the security pipeline across 30+ microservices
- Automate vulnerability scanning and reporting
- Partner with development teams to remediate findings
- Design and implement security monitoring and alerting
- Lead incident response for security events
- Mentor junior engineers on security best practices
